Nearly all boundary layers begin laminar, but at sufficient distance downstream nearly all of them are turbulent. So the question is - how do we get from one to the other?
The answer is **transition** but this process is:
- very complex
- not very well understood
And as a result we shall treat it largely empirically and qualitatively.
