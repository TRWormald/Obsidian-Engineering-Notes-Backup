A control volume is a volume of a fluid that exhibits several characteristics:
1) Conservation of Mass
2) Conservation of Momentum
### Control Volume Conservation of Mass
>This is a continuity relation - what goes in, must come out, or accumulate in the control volume (i.e. density increases).
