### The Units

| Basic Unit  | SI  | Imperial |
| ----------- | --- | -------- |
| Mass        | kg  | slug     |
| Length      | m   | ft       |
| Time        | s   | s        |
| Temperature | C/K | F/R      |

| Derived Units  | SI   | Imperial |
| -------------- | ---- | -------- |
| Force          | N/kN | lbf      |
| Pressure       | kPa  | psi      |
| Work or Energy | kJ   | ft lb    |
| Power          | kW   | hp       |
### Symbols
Mass Flow $\dot{m}$ (kg/s)
Thrust/Force $F$ (kN)
Absolute Velocity $C$ (m/s)
Relative Velocity $V$ (m/s)
Mach Number $M$
Total Temperature $T_{0}$ (C or K)
Static Temperature $T$ (C or K)
Total Pressure $P_{0}$ (kPa / bar)
